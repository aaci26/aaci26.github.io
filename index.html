Inspired by these experiences, I am firmly committed to becoming a licensed Marriage and Family Therapist, with a focus on serving youth and young adults. As a fourth-year Psychology major at the University of California, Riverside, I have already gained practical skills working both within academic settings and in the broader community. Through every step, I remain grateful for the journey thus far and hopeful for the impact I can have as I move forward.
